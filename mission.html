Vision:

Our vision is a future where girls of color are well-represented and thriving in the field of technology. We envision a world where diversity and inclusion are celebrated, and every young woman has the opportunity to reach her full potential in STEM fields.

Values:

  • Inclusivity: We believe in creating a supportive and inclusive environment where girls of all backgrounds feel welcome and valued.
  • Empowerment: We empower girls to take ownership of their education and career paths, providing them with the tools and resources they need to succeed.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, from our educational programs to our mentorship initiatives, to ensure the highest quality outcomes for our participants.

Goals and Objectives:

  • Increase Participation: Expand access to computer science education and mentorship opportunities for girls of color, particularly those from underrepresented communities.
  • Foster Leadership: Develop leadership skills and confidence in young women to become advocates for diversity and inclusion in the tech industry.
  • Drive Impact: Measure and track the impact of our programs by monitoring participant outcomes and success stories.

Impact Stories:

  • Meet Maria: Maria joined Tech Bridges as a high school student with a passion for coding but limited access to resources. Through our mentorship program, she gained hands-on experience, built a strong network of support, and ultimately secured a full scholarship to pursue computer science at her dream university.
  • Success Story: Tech Bridges alumna, Jessica, now works as a software engineer at a leading tech company, where she is using her skills to develop innovative solutions and mentor the next generation of young women in tech.

Get Involved:

  • Volunteer: Join our team of volunteers and mentors to make a difference in the lives of young women in tech.
  • Donate: Support our mission by making a donation to fund scholarships, educational programs, and resources for girls of color in computer science.
  • Spread the Word: Share our mission with your network and help us reach more girls who can benefit from our programs.

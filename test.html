About Tech Bridges

Tech Bridges is a community-driven initiative dedicated to empowering and inspiring girls of color to pursue careers in computer science and technology. We believe that diversity drives innovation, and we're committed to creating a more inclusive and equitable tech industry.

Our Services

  • Access to Mentorship Programs: Connect with experienced mentors who can provide guidance and support on your journey in computer science.
  • Online Learning Resources: Access curated lists of online courses, tutorials, and coding resources tailored to beginners in computer science.

Get Involved

Ready to embark on your journey in computer science? Join our community today and unlock a world of opportunities! Connect with mentors, access resources, and become part of a supportive network of girls of color in tech.
